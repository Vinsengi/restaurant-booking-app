About Chez Mama

Welcome to Chez Mama — where passion meets the plate.

Founded with love and tradition, Chez Mama is a cozy, family-owned restaurant inspired by the warm hospitality of African and French kitchens. Our dishes are crafted from fresh, local ingredients, blending home-cooked comfort with rich culinary heritage.

Whether you're sharing stories over a plate of grilled fish or celebrating a special moment, Chez Mama is your home away from home.
